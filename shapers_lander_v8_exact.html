The application process

1

The initial application

Fill out a quick online form to confirm your eligibility (country, age, and availability).

2

The assessments

If you meet the basic criteria, you'll receive two assessments:

The self-assessment — A look at your working style, motivations, and how you fit into a team. No right or wrong answers.

The problem-solving challenge — This is where you prove you belong in the top 1%. Past experience is great, but adaptability is better. This challenge strips away the CV and tests your raw ability to research, connect the dots, and tackle real-life scenarios.

3

The vibe check (institute interview)

A quick, relaxed chat with our team. We already know you have the skills — now we just want to meet the human behind them. We want to hear about your goals and make sure the program is a genuine fit.

The final step: The match (company interview)

Meet your potential host company. At this stage it's about finding the right mutual fit. Nail this, and you've officially claimed one of the 12 spots.

FAQ

F.A.Q.

Is there a catch? Do I have to pay for the Academy?
No. The 12 spots are fully funded scholarships.
What is the monthly stipend?
The financial support is designed to fully cover your day-to-day living costs in Ljubljana — rent, groceries, and more. Exact figures are shared with accepted participants.
Will the financial support cover my living expenses?
Yes. The financial support during the 6-month internship is designed to cover day-to-day living costs (rent, groceries, etc.) and more.
Does the Academy provide housing?
No. The Institute does not own dorms (yet), but will 100% help you sort out the living situation prior to your arrival if selected. Average rent in Ljubljana is €300–500/month for a room in a shared flat.
Which regions can apply?
Europe, USA, Canada, United Kingdom, Singapore.
Do I need a visa?
Not if you can travel freely within Europe without a visa. If applying from the USA, UK, Canada, or Singapore, our team will guide you through visa and legal requirements once officially selected.
Can I apply if I'm over 30?
No, in most cases — however exceptions can be made for exceptional applicants.
Is prior experience in Marketing, Sales, or Business Development required?
No. We look for potential, mindset, grit, and the ability to think outside the box. The 4-week intensive sprint is designed to get you market-ready.
Can I do this alongside my degree or another job?
Only if your current commitments are fully remote and you're a productivity monster. Otherwise, no. This is fully on-site in Ljubljana.
What kind of companies will I be working with?
Dynamic companies across various industries that align with our core value of empowering young talent — ready to give you real responsibility, high-stakes problems, and a seat at the table from day one.
What happens if there is no fit with the company?
You retain your scholarship and status as an accepted candidate. The moment a new spot opens in our partner network, you'll be next in line.
